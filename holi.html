Our Story

Born from a family recipe passed down through generations, Mohabbat Ki Sharbat brings the authentic taste of traditional Indian refreshment with a modern twist.

Our special blend of rose, cardamom, and secret ingredients creates a cooling sensation that's perfect for the vibrant Holi celebrations.

Each glass is crafted with love and served with a sprinkle of happiness, making it not just a drink but an experience to remember.
